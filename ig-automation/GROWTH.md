# VibeCodeWeb Instagram — Organic Growth Playbook

No shortcuts. Follower-buying, follow/unfollow bots, and auto-DM tools get accounts
shadowbanned or suspended (Meta enforcement tightened in late 2025). This is the compliant
playbook the automation supports — the system handles content + publishing + insights; the
human/relationship parts below are what actually compound followers.

## The growth loop
1. **Post consistently** — ~1 feed/day (auto), 3–4 Reels/week, daily Stories.
2. **Reels are the engine** — they out-reach static posts for discovery. Prioritize them.
3. **Hook in the first 1–2 seconds / first line** — a question or bold claim.
4. **One clear CTA per post** — "DM AUTO", "link in bio", "comment 1".
5. **Reply within the first hour** — early engagement signals reach. Answer every comment/DM.
6. **Read insights weekly** (`insights.php`) → double down on top formats + best posting times.

## What moves the needle (do these manually)
- **Engage outward 15 min/day**: thoughtful comments on India SMB / startup / founder posts.
  Real comments > follows. Never automate this.
- **Collaborate**: collab posts/Reels with complementary local businesses or creators.
- **Cross-promote**: share posts to WhatsApp status; the site footer now links to Instagram;
  add the IG handle to your email signature and WhatsApp Business profile.
- **DMs the right way**: only reply to people who message first, or warm contacts. No cold mass-DM.

## Posting times (starting guess, then tune from insights)
- Weekdays **10:30** and **19:30 IST** tend to work for Indian SMB audiences.
- After 2 weeks, let `insights.php` data pick the real best slots.

## Hashtags
- Rotate the 3 sets (A: AI, B: product, C: India SMB) — defined in `seed_content.php` / the kit.
- 15–25 relevant tags; avoid banned/spammy tags; refresh the sets monthly.

## Reels (the honest gap)
High-quality video can't be fully auto-generated yet. Options, easiest first:
- **Templated motion graphics** from the branded image templates (text + simple animation).
- **Screen-recordings** of builds / dashboards with a voiceover or captions.
- **Your phone clips** (10–30s) — drop them in and we caption + schedule them.
